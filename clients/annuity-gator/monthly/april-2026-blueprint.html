This Month's Trust Strategy
Month 1:

Close the Scheduling Gap

Annuity Gator has 40% open rates and 0.15% click-through rates. People are reading every email, but almost nobody clicks. The problem isn't engagement or deliverability. The problem is structural: emails read like educational newsletters with a single buried CTA. Readers enjoy the content and leave. This month, we keep the calm advisor tone that works, but we restructure every email to create clear movement toward scheduling. Shorter copy, multiple CTAs woven throughout, and specific language about what the call actually involves. The ask stays the same: schedule your free 15-minute evaluation. The framing changes based on where the reader is in their journey.

Account Diagnosis

Where Things Stand

01

The Scheduling Gap

Lead-to-appointment conversion is 37-41% (goal: 50-60%+). Keap tag "02. Requested Annuity Evaluation - Didn't Schedule" identifies the gap. These people completed the quiz and requested an evaluation but never booked a call. This is the highest-leverage segment in the entire list.

02

Email Structure Problem

Current emails are 150-250 words with a single CTA buried mid-body. They read like educational articles, not conversion emails. Open rates are excellent (40%), but CTR is 0.15%. The fix is structural: shorter copy (50-100 words), 3 CTAs per email, and hooks that create momentum toward scheduling.

03

Automation Gap

Only 53 contacts are active across all automations for a 30K list. No welcome sequence, no "didn't schedule" recovery flow, no re-engagement campaign. This blueprint introduces 4 automation flows that will run 24/7 once activated.

04

CPL Pressure

Meta CPL is running $319/lead (target: $250). Improving email conversion doesn't reduce CPL directly, but it means each lead generates more appointments, improving the effective cost per appointment significantly. Getting 50-60% conversion from the same leads is like cutting CPL in half.

Monthly Strategy

The April Approach

01

Primary Goal

Move lead-to-appointment conversion from 37-41% toward 50%+. Every email, automation, and campaign in this blueprint exists to get more people to schedule their free 15-minute evaluation call.

02

Voice Calibration

Keep the calm, empathetic advisor tone. Keep "No pressure. No obligation. Just clarity." Keep the heavy line breaks. Change the structure: shorter copy, 3 CTAs per email, hooks that drive action instead of education. Every email sent from the assigned advisor, not from the Annuity Gator brand.

03

Trust Architecture

Two value deposits before one direct ask. Campaigns follow the Gain/Logic/Fear sequence: Email 1 leads with outcome, Email 2 leads with proof, Email 3 leads with consequence of inaction. All urgency is life-stage based, never artificial.

04

Key Metrics

Target open rate: maintain 35%+. Target CTR: 1%+ (up from 0.15%). Target lead-to-appointment: 50%+. Unsubscribe ceiling: 0.3% per send.
