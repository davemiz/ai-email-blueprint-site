Why This Matters

Every Job Should Lead to the Next One

Right now, when Anyclean finishes a job, nothing happens. The customer is happy, the home is clean, and then... silence. No prompt to rebook. No referral ask. No reminder that you exist.

Word of mouth is already your best channel. Your repeat customers are your most profitable ones. This system turns every completed job into a machine that generates reviews, referrals, and repeat bookings automatically.

This extends the existing Review Request automation (2 emails, already live) by adding 5 more emails to the same "Job Completed" trigger. The full flow becomes: feedback check (Day 1), review ask (Day 4), referral ask (Day 7), rebook seed (Day 10), second referral touch (Day 21), regular service pitch (Day 30), and seasonal rebook (Day 90). One automation, seven emails, running continuously after every job.

Referral Programme

How the Referral Incentive Works

01

Keep it dead simple

No referral codes, no apps, no tracking links. The customer just tells their friend to mention their name when booking. Anyclean's office team notes the referral and applies the discounts manually. Friction kills referrals. The simpler the process, the more people do it.

02

10% off for both parties

The referred friend gets 10% off their first booking. The referrer gets 10% off their next booking. Both sides benefit. The discount is modest enough to protect margins but meaningful enough to motivate action.

03

Track referrals in a simple spreadsheet

Create a shared Google Sheet with columns: Referrer Name, Referrer Email, Referred Name, Date, Booking Value, Discount Applied. Review monthly. This gives you data on which customers refer most (your best advocates) and the revenue generated from referrals.

MailerLite Setup

How to Build This

01

Open the existing Review Request automation

In MailerLite, find the automation triggered by the "Job Completed" group. It currently has 2 emails: "How Was Your Clean?" (Day 1) and "Quick Favour?" (Day 4). We're adding 5 more emails to the end of this flow.

02

Add the 5 new emails with delays

After the existing Email 02 (Day 4): add a 3-day delay, then Email 03 (referral). Add a 3-day delay, then Email 04 (rebook seed). Add an 11-day delay, then Email 05 (second referral). Add a 9-day delay, then Email 06 (one-month pitch). Add a 60-day delay, then Email 07 (seasonal rebook).

03

Copy in the email content

Use the copy buttons above to grab each email's content. Paste into MailerLite's rich text editor. All emails use the {$name} merge tag. Make sure CTA buttons link to https://anyclean.co.uk/get-a-quote.

04

Activate and confirm the trigger method

The automation is already triggered by the "Job Completed" group. Confirm with the client: are they manually adding customers to this group after each job, or using a Zapier integration from ServiceCEO? Either works, but consistency matters. Every completed job should trigger this flow.
