Testing Strategy

A/B Tests Built Into Every Send

Every new campaign has subject line tests baked in. Here's what we're testing this month and why.

01

Question Hook vs. Benefit Statement

Campaign 4 (Easter): Subject A ("Easter's coming, is your house ready?") vs Subject C ("Four days off, zero cleaning"). Testing whether a question or a benefit statement drives more opens on seasonal content.

02

Aspirational vs. Specific

Campaign 5 (Regular Cleaning): Subject A ("What if you never had to clean again?") vs Subject C ("Saturday morning, no hoovering"). Does a big aspirational question or a specific relatable moment get more engagement?

03

Friendly vs. Direct

Campaign 5 (Trial): Subject A ("Try us once, see what you think") vs Subject C ("One clean, no contract"). Testing warm-casual against direct-clear for a commitment-lowering email.

04

Specific Detail vs. Seasonal

Campaign 6 (Outdoor): Subject A ("Your windows are dirtier than you think") vs Subject C ("Spring sunshine shows everything"). Testing a specific provocation against a broader seasonal observation.

How to run in MailerLite: When creating each campaign, enable "A/B split testing" in the campaign settings. Choose "Subject line" as the variable. Set a 50/50 split. Send to 30% of the audience first, wait 3 hours, then MailerLite automatically sends the winning subject to the remaining 70%. Log winners in the performance log for May planning.
