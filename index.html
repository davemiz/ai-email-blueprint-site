02
The CTA Problem

YOU'RE ASKING FOR THE SALE BEFORE YOU'VE EARNED IT.

Think about the last five emails you sent.

How many of them asked for money?

All of them? Most of them?

Yeah. That's the problem.

Someone opts in on Tuesday. By Thursday you're hitting them with "BUY NOW — $2,997."

They've read two emails. They don't know you. They don't trust you.

And you're asking for three grand.

That's not a call to action. That's a proposal on the first date.

No wonder they ghosted.

Every ask costs trust. If you haven't earned it, they're gone.

Changing the button color isn't going to fix this.

You have to know where your reader is in the relationship and make the ask that matches THAT moment. Not the moment you wish they were at.

A brand new subscriber and someone who's bought from you three times? Those are completely different conversations. You're having the same one with both of them.
