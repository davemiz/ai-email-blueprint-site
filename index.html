The Problem

This is why your email isn't making you the money it should.

You're stuck in one of these three traps. Pick yours.

01

The "we'll get to it" trap

Someone on your team "owns" email. Emails go out when there's time. Strategy is "send something and hope." No testing, no segmentation, no plan.

Result: Email stays a side project. Revenue stays flat. Your list gets colder every week you wait.
02

The "just use AI" trap

You paste a prompt into ChatGPT, get polished-sounding copy, and hit send. It reads like every other brand in the inbox. Generic tone, no strategy, no segmentation.

Result: Copy that sounds good but doesn't convert. No deliverability thinking. Revenue per send stays flat.
03

The "expensive agency" trap

You pay $3K to $15K a month, hand over the keys, and hope they "get it." Your voice turns into funnel-bro copy. Most of the work gets pushed to a junior.

Result: High cost, low control. When they leave, your entire email strategy walks out the door with them.

The problem isn't effort. It's that none of these were built to think about your email revenue every single day. You need a system that does.

If you're just starting and barely have a list, you don't need this yet. If you've got buyers and traffic, but email is clearly underperforming, you're exactly who this is for.
